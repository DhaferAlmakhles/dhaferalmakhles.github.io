--- layout: archive title: "Teaching" permalink: /teaching/ author_profile: true --- Listed are the courses I have taught at Prince Sultan University. CME343 Communication Systems I: This course introduces the fundamental principles of communication systems. It covers topics such as modulation techniques, signal processing, and the analysis of communication signals and systems​​. **ETHC305 Ethical & Social Aspects of Engineering:** This course explores the ethical, legal, and social implications of engineering practice. It addresses issues such as professional responsibility, ethical decision-making, and the impact of engineering solutions on society and the environment. **CME492 CO-OP**: This cooperative education course provides students with practical experience in an industrial setting. It aims to integrate academic learning with real-world work experience, allowing students to apply their knowledge and skills in a professional environment​​. **EE231 Signals and Systems:** This course covers the analysis of signals and systems in both continuous and discrete time. Topics include Fourier series and transforms, Laplace transforms, and the basics of linear system theory. **CME112 Electric Circuit and Electronics:** This introductory course focuses on the principles of electric circuits and basic electronics. Students learn about circuit components, network theorems, and the analysis of DC and AC circuits​. **CME342 Communication Theory**: This course delves into the theoretical foundations of communication systems, including information theory, coding, and modulation techniques. It emphasizes the mathematical modeling and performance analysis of communication systems​. **CME241 Signals and Systems**: This course provides a comprehensive study of signals and systems, including time-domain and frequency-domain analysis, system properties, and the use of transforms for system analysis. **EE332 Control Systems**: This course introduces the principles of control systems engineering. Topics include system modeling, feedback control, stability analysis, and the design of control systems using various methods such as PID controllers. **EE492 Cooperative Training**: This is another cooperative education course designed to give students hands-on experience in the industry.
